About Me

My name is Sean King, welcome to my portfolio page.

I have been married for nearly five years to by beautiful wife Sarah, and we have a little girl who is two and a half years old named Gwendolyn.
Last year I graduated from the University of Utah with a Bachelor's of Science in Health, Society, and Policy, which is a program dedicated to studying how different laws affect the general health of the populations in that governance.
Using that degree I earned at the university, I am currently working as an employee with the State of Utah's Department of Workforce Services determining eligibility for others to receive welfare based benefits. I am also working right now a part time job with an escape room company called Getout Games which is one of the more entertaining jobs I've had in my life.

Right now I am also enrolled in the University of Utah's Coding Bootcamp to learn both the front and back ends of website development. The purpose of this portfolio is to show off what I have learned in that class and prepare myself for employment.
